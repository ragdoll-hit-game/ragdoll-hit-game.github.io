At ragdoll-hit-game.github.io, we hold the intellectual property rights of creators, copyright holders, and licensors in the highest regard. We are fully committed to complying with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and all applicable copyright laws and regulations to ensure a lawful and fair environment on our platform.

We take copyright infringement seriously and promptly respond to all legitimate claims. When a valid notice is received, we act quickly to remove or disable access to the infringing content to protect the rights of the original owners and maintain the integrity of our website.

Our dedication to upholding intellectual property rights supports a creative and respectful community where original works are valued and protected.

How to Report Copyright Infringement

If you believe that content on ragdoll-hit-game.github.io infringes your copyright, we urge you to notify us immediately. To ensure an efficient and proper response under the DMCA, please provide a written notification containing the following detailed information:

  • Your full legal name, complete mailing address, telephone number, and email address for communication.

  • A clear description of the copyrighted work(s) you believe have been infringed, including registration or documentation details if available.

  • The exact URL(s) on ragdoll-hit-game.github.io where the allegedly infringing material is located.

  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, declaring that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is unauthorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

  • A statement confirming that the information in your notification is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

  • Your physical or electronic signature to authenticate the notice.

Please send your complete copyright infringement notice to our designated DMCA agent listed below.

Counter-Notification Procedure

If content has been removed or access disabled due to a copyright claim but you believe this was done in error or due to misidentification, you may file a counter-notification. Your counter-notice must include:

  • Your full legal name, current mailing address, telephone number, and email address.

  • Identification of the material that was removed or disabled and the URL where it was located before removal.

  • A statement made under penalty of perjury asserting your good faith belief that the material was removed or disabled due to a mistake or misidentification.

  • Consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court in your jurisdiction (or another appropriate court) and agreement to accept service of process from the original complaining party.

  • Your handwritten or electronic signature to validate the counter-notice.

Upon receiving a valid counter-notice, we will promptly forward it to the original claimant. Unless a lawsuit is filed within the statutory period, we will restore the content in compliance with DMCA requirements.

Policy on Repeat Infringers

ragdoll-hit-game.github.io enforces a strict policy against repeat copyright infringement. Users or accounts found to be repeatedly infringing copyrights may have their access suspended or permanently terminated according to DMCA guidelines and our internal policies.

This measure is vital to maintaining a fair, respectful, and legally compliant community that honors creators’ rights.
