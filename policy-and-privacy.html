Privacy Policy

Introduction

At ragdoll-hit-game.github.io, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and your rights regarding your data when you interact with our website. We are committed to maintaining the security and confidentiality of your personal data and ensuring transparency about our data handling practices.

This policy applies whenever you visit or use services provided by ragdoll-hit-game.github.io and is designed to comply with relevant data protection regulations.

Information We Collect

To enhance your experience and deliver our services effectively, we may collect the following categories of information:

Personal Information

This includes any data you voluntarily provide to us during interactions such as account registration, newsletter subscription, feedback submission, or customer support inquiries. Examples of personal information include:

  • Your full name

  • Email address

  • Contact details

  • Any other data you provide voluntarily through forms or communications

Technical Information

We automatically collect technical details about your device and browsing behavior to improve website functionality and security. This information may include:

  • IP address

  • Browser type and version

  • Operating system

  • Device identifiers

This data is collected using cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies.

Usage Information

We gather data about how you interact with ragdoll-hit-game.github.io to better understand user behavior and preferences. This includes:

  • Frequency and duration of visits

  • Navigation paths across pages

  • Interaction with specific content or games

These insights help us refine our platform to enhance usability and performance.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is utilized for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Operating, maintaining, and improving the website and gaming services we provide.

  • Communicating with you regarding updates, promotions, newsletters, and responding to your inquiries or feedback.

  • Personalizing content and game recommendations to suit your interests and preferences.

  • Monitoring usage patterns to enhance site performance, security, and user experience.

  • Ensuring compliance with legal obligations and protecting against fraud or abuse.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

ragdoll-hit-game.github.io uses cookies and related technologies to collect information that helps us deliver a better browsing experience. Cookies enable us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.

You have control over cookie settings through your browser preferences. Please note that disabling cookies may limit some functionalities of the website.

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the website, conducting analytics, or providing customer support. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and may only use your data to perform services on our behalf.

In certain cases, we may disclose personal information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or governmental request. We may also share data to protect our rights, safety, or property and that of our users.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure servers, access controls, and regular security assessments.

However, please understand that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, where legally permitted.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond promptly and in accordance with applicable laws.
